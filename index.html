HEAR IT FROM THEM

QUESTIONS?

Everything you need to know about the process.

What's your creative philosophy?

+
My goal is simple: to create work that is designed to be remembered. I focus on retention, emotional impact, and narrative flow rather than just flashy transitions. Every cut serves the story.

Do you sign NDAs?

+
Yes, absolutely. I regularly work with high-profile clients and confidential launches. I am happy to sign an NDA before we begin any discussions.

How many revisions do you offer?

+
My standard package includes 2 rounds of creative revisions. I find this strikes the perfect balance for collaboration suitable for most projects. Additional rounds can be added if needed.

What's your turnaround time?

+
For short-form content (Reels, TikToks), turnaround is typically 1-3 business days. For long-form (YouTube), allow 3-7 business days depending on complexity and raw footage length.

Do you work alone or with a team?

+
The creative heavy lifting—cutting, story, grading—is all me. I want personal control over the quality. I do utilize junior editors for organisation and sync workflows to keep things moving fast.

FAQ

COMMON
QUESTIONS

Everything you need to know before we start working together.

What's your creative philosophy?

To create content that's designed to be remembered — not to be a part of the scroll. Every edit I deliver is intentional: the pacing, the cuts, the color grading — all built to hold attention and leave a mark. Forgettable content is noise. I'm not interested in making noise.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes, absolutely — when required. I take client confidentiality seriously. If your project involves sensitive brand assets, unreleased content, or proprietary strategy, I'm fully open to signing an NDA before we begin. Just bring it up during our call.

How many revisions do you offer?

My default is 2 rounds of revisions per video — which covers the vast majority of projects. That said, every collaboration is different. Revision rounds are fully customisable and can be agreed upfront based on project scope and complexity.

What's your turnaround time?

Short-form (Reels, Shorts, TikToks): 1–3 days. Long-form (YouTube, brand films, documentaries): 3–7 days. Rush deliveries can be arranged. I'll always give you a clear timeline at the start of each project — no surprises.

Do you work alone or with a team?

All creative decisions — storytelling, pacing, color, cuts — are 100% mine. For non-creative workflow tasks I work with 1–2 junior editors and trusted freelancers for complex technical elements. But quality control never leaves my hands. What you approve, I've personally reviewed.
